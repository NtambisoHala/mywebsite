Deadpool & Wolverine

Deadpool and Wolverine (D&W) is a movie that brings together two popular Marvel characters in an action-packed story. Now, I'm not gonna spoil too much but, the film starts with Deadpool teaming up with Wolverine to fight a powerful mutant threat. The actors, Ryan Reynolds as deadpool and Hugh Jackman as Wolverine, have good chemistry and bring their characters to life well.

However, the movie falls short in some areas. The plot is not very exciting and the supporting characters are not well-developed. The action scenes are well done but can feel repetitive. Some of the jokes also don't land and feel...forced.

On the positive side, the movie has great special effects, making Deadpool and Wolverine's powers look impressive. The supportive actors, like Rebecca Ferguson, also give good performances.Â 

Overall, D&W is a mixed bag. Fans of the characters may still enjoy seeing them team up, but the movie doesn't fully live up to its potential. It has some exciting moments and humour, but it may not satisfy everyone.

5/10, wouldn't watch again.
